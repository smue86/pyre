PYRE Image Assets
=================

Place your product images in the following folders:

/product/
  - hero.png (or .jpg) - Main hero product shot (recommended: 2000x2000px)
  - product-angle-1.png - Alternate angle 1
  - product-angle-2.png - Alternate angle 2
  - product-angle-3.png - Alternate angle 3

/technology/
  - aerocore-structure.png - Ceramic foam visualization
  - airflow-diagram.png - Convection system diagram
  - temperature-graph.png - Temperature efficiency chart

/modules/
  - pellet-feeder.png - Pellet system image
  - flattop.png - Flat top griddle
  - pizza-oven.png - Pizza oven module
  - rotisserie.png - Rotisserie system
  - offset-box.png - Offset firebox
  - cold-smoke.png - Cold smoke generator

/lifestyle/
  - outdoor-cooking.jpg - Lifestyle shot with PYRE in use
  - brisket-cook.jpg - Food photography
  - chef-using.jpg - Chef using the product

Recommended formats: WebP or PNG for product shots, JPG for lifestyle
Recommended resolution: 2x for retina displays
